Frequently Asked Questions

What is IEEE CIS CUSB?
IEEE CIS CUSB is the Computational Intelligence Society student branch at Chandigarh University, focused on AI, machine learning, and computational intelligence activities, workshops, and events.
Who can join the club?
Any student of Chandigarh University interested in computational intelligence, AI, or related fields can join the club.
How do I become a member?
Click the “Apply for Membership” button on the homepage and fill out the registration form. You will be contacted by the club for further steps.
What kind of events does the club organize?
We organize workshops, hackathons, seminars, guest lectures, and hands-on sessions on AI, ML, and computational intelligence topics.
Is there a membership fee?
Membership is usually free for most events, but to supoort ieee-cis-cusb there is a membership fee of 1800 rupees.
How can I contact the club?
Use the “Contact” button in the footer to message us directly on WhatsApp, or connect with us via our social media links.
Where can I find updates about upcoming events?
All updates are posted on the website, and you can also follow our official social media handles for the latest news.
