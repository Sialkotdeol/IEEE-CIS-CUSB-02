About the Website
This website is the official portal for the IEEE CIS CUSB student branch at Chandigarh University. It provides information about the club, its members, events, and resources for students interested in computational intelligence and AI.
How to Join
To join the club, click the “Apply for Membership” button on the homepage and fill out the registration form. Club coordinators will contact you with further details.
Events & Activities
The club organizes workshops, hackathons, seminars, and guest lectures on AI, ML, and computational intelligence. Check the Past Events section for highlights and updates.
Contact & Support
For any queries, use the “Contact” button in the footer to reach us on WhatsApp, or connect via our social media links.
Contributing
If you wish to contribute to the website or club activities, please reach out to the club coordinators or submit a pull request on our GitHub repository.
